About

Illinois Covid Modeling is a joint effort between Northwestern University and the University of Chicago, who have been modeling COVID-19 in Illinois since March 2020. In partnership with the Illinois Department of Public Health (IDPH), we use simulation models to predict trends in hospital usage and deaths at the COVID-19 Region level. Both models are SEIR-based compartmental models that are calibrated to daily hospital census, ICU census, and death data collected by IDPH, and both teams have created a suite of 11 models, one for each COVID-19 Region.

Code for University of Chicago's simulation model is available here.

Code for Northwestern's simulation model is available here. Northwestern is estimating Rt by applying the EpiEstim package to the timeseries of daily new infections inferred from the simulation models.

Case and death data shown on the landing page are aggregated from county-level public data from IDPH .
